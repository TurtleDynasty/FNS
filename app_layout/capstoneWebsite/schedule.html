Development Plan

Current Status

As shown in the figure above team FNS is currently approaching finishing up the final requirements of the Data Visualization project. We have gone through all of the information produced from the Spectrum Protect servers and found all relevant and imperative information that is necessary to analyze when managing your data on the Spectrum Protect server.
